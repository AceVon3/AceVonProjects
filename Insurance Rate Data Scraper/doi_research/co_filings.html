Insurance Plan Filings & Approved Plans
Filings are the plan and premium requests from the companies that are reviewed by the Division. Colorado utilizes the National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC) System for Electronic Rate and Form Filing (SERFF) Access interface.
1

Insurance Plan Filings & Approved Plans

Search and Review Insurance Company Filings

What type of insurance filings can be accessed?

Property and Casualty

Filings for property and casualty insurance include, but are not limited to, the following lines of insurance:  personal automobile; homeowners; workers’ compensation; commercial liability; title; and commercial automobile.

Life, Accident and Health

Filings for life, accident and health insurance include, but are not limited to, the following lines of insurance:  life, health benefit plans (HMO, EPO, PPO and major medical), Supplement (such as Medicare Supplement and Tricare Supplement), Long Term Care, Disability Income, Stop Loss (Health), Viatical Settlements and Limited Benefit (such as Accident, Dread Disease, Hospital Indemnity) and all other lines of health coverage.

Access and Review Company Filings

View Filings via SERFF System

  • Step-by-Step guide on accessing insurance filings and submitting comments on insurance filings - Revised 2024
  • If you have limited access to the internet, you can look up filings using the computer kiosks available in DORA's Welcome Center at 1560 Broadway, in the main lobby of the building.
  • PLEASE NOTE: Filings can be 100+ pages and are very technical. Filings may contain or incorporate material that is protected by U.S. or foreign copyright laws, and no person may reproduce, redistribute, or make commercial use of any such material in violation of those laws. This includes, but is not limited to AAIS, ISO, and other rating organizations’ forms which are copyrighted and are not to be copied or reproduced without their permission.
